About Hit Play

Hit Play is a punk rock band born in the heart of Miami, FL. Known for blistering riffs, high-energy performances, and a fiercely loyal fanbase, we combine classic punk roots with modern intensity.

We’ve played stages big and small, from sweaty club shows to festival crowds, always bringing raw energy and a sound that hits fast and hits hard.

Whether we're on stage or in the studio, our mission is the same: keep it loud, keep it real, and always hit play.
